## Troubleshooting

**Extension won't load?**
- Make sure you selected the `voice starter` folder (contains `manifest.json`)
- Check for errors in `chrome://extensions/`

**Side panel blank?**
- Right-click in panel ‚Üí Inspect ‚Üí Check console for errors

**Recording doesn't work?**
- Microphone permission granted?
- Check service worker logs: click "service worker" link in `chrome://extensions/`
